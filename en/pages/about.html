About Us

Al-Hassan Law Firm

Al-Hassan Law Firm was established in 2022 in Al-Ahsa Governorate, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, with the aim of providing distinguished legal services that meet the needs of clients. The firm includes a select group of experienced and competent lawyers and legal consultants in various legal fields.

We always strive to provide innovative and effective legal solutions that contribute to achieving our clients' goals and protecting their interests. We believe that success is achieved by building long-term relationships with clients based on trust, transparency, and commitment.

Our firm is characterized by high professionalism, precision in work, and commitment to professional ethics, making us the ideal choice for clients seeking professional legal services.

Our Vision

To be the leading law firm in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, and a model to be followed in providing distinguished legal services that meet clients' needs and exceed their expectations.

Our Mission

To provide professional and innovative legal services that contribute to achieving our clients' goals and protecting their interests, through a distinguished team of experienced and competent lawyers and legal consultants.

Our Values
