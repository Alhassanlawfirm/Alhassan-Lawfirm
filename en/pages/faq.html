Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our legal services

What legal services does Al-Hassan Law Firm provide?

Al-Hassan Law Firm offers a wide range of legal services including legal consultations, litigation and disputes, bankruptcy applications, contract drafting and review, intellectual property registration, foreign investment and company establishment, legal management for enterprises, debt collection, franchise agreements, and documentation services. You can view details of each service on our Services page.

How can I book a legal consultation with one of the firm's lawyers?

You can book a legal consultation through several methods: via our website through the "Book a Legal Consultation" page, by calling directly at +966 50 316 9240, or by sending an email to Info@alhassanlawfirm.com. We will contact you to schedule the consultation, whether in-person at our office or remotely via video conferencing.

What is the cost of a legal consultation?

The cost of legal consultation varies depending on the type, nature, and duration of the consultation. Simple initial consultations may be free in some cases, while specialized and detailed consultations range from 500 to 1,500 Saudi Riyals. You can inquire about the specific cost when booking a consultation. We also offer monthly consultation packages for companies and institutions at competitive prices.

What documents are required to file a lawsuit?

The required documents for filing a lawsuit vary depending on the type and subject of the case. Generally, they include: national ID, residency permit, or commercial registration of the plaintiff; supporting documents (contracts, correspondence, receipts, reports, photos, etc.); notarized power of attorney (if appointing a lawyer); and any other documents related to the case. Our lawyer will specify the exact required documents after studying your case.

Does the firm provide legal services for individuals or only for companies?

Al-Hassan Law Firm provides its legal services to both individuals and companies/institutions alike. We handle individual cases in various areas such as personal status, labor, real estate, and commercial matters. We also provide specialized services for companies and institutions in investment, company establishment, commercial contracts, intellectual property protection, and more.

How are legal fees calculated for cases?

Legal fees are calculated in several ways depending on the nature, complexity, and expected duration of the case. Common methods include: fixed fees (a specific amount for the entire case), hourly fees (a set rate per hour of legal work), or a percentage of the claim value (especially in collection and financial claims cases). In all cases, fees are agreed upon clearly and in writing before starting work on the case.

Can I get a remote legal consultation?

Yes, Al-Hassan Law Firm offers remote legal consultation services via video conferencing tools such as Zoom, Microsoft Teams, or by phone. This service is available to clients inside and outside the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, providing flexibility and convenience while maintaining the quality of legal consultation. You can book a remote consultation through our website or by contacting the office directly.

What is the expected duration of litigation for cases?

The duration of litigation varies depending on the type and complexity of the case and the competent court. Generally, simple cases may take 3 to 6 months, while complex cases may last a year or more, especially if the judgment is appealed. At Al-Hassan Law Firm, we always strive to expedite litigation procedures as much as possible while maintaining quality legal representation, and we provide clients with a realistic estimate of the expected duration based on our experience with similar cases.

Does the firm offer trademark registration services?

Yes, Al-Hassan Law Firm provides trademark registration and protection services in Saudi Arabia, Gulf countries, Arab countries, and internationally. We are accredited agents of the Saudi Authority for Intellectual Property and offer comprehensive services including searching for similar marks, preparing registration applications, following up procedures with relevant authorities, responding to objections, trademark renewals, and pursuing trademark infringement cases.

What legal management services for enterprises does the firm offer?

Legal management services for enterprises offered by Al-Hassan Law Firm include: reviewing and drafting contracts and agreements, providing daily legal consultations, preparing and updating internal regulations and policies, representing the enterprise in legal disputes, dealing with government and regulatory bodies, protecting intellectual property rights, assisting in compliance with systems and regulations, and providing legal training for employees. We offer these services through annual or monthly contracts according to the enterprise's needs.

Are there fees for attending court sessions?

Usually, fees for attending court sessions are included in the legal fees agreed upon in the engagement contract. In some cases, especially if the court is in a different city from the office location, there may be additional fees to cover travel and accommodation costs. All these details are clarified in the engagement contract before starting work on the case to ensure complete transparency.

Can I get a free legal consultation?

In some cases, we offer free initial and brief legal consultations to assess the case and determine initial steps. These free consultations are limited in scope and duration, aiming to help the client understand their general legal situation. For detailed and comprehensive legal consultation, fees are determined based on its nature and duration. We also provide pro bono legal services in some humanitarian and charitable cases as part of our social responsibility.
