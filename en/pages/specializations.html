Our Legal Specializations

We specialize in providing legal services across a diverse range of sectors to meet our clients' needs

Industry & Energy

We provide specialized legal services in the industry and energy sector, including legal consultations, contract drafting, and dispute resolution related to energy and industrial projects.

Entertainment & Cinema

We specialize in providing legal services to the entertainment and cinema sector, including intellectual property rights, production and distribution contracts, licensing, and protection of copyright and artists' rights.

Real Estate & Contracting

We provide comprehensive legal services in real estate and contracting, including drafting and reviewing sale, purchase, lease, and contracting agreements, resolving real estate disputes, and legal consultations related to real estate development.

Finance & Banking

We specialize in providing legal services to the financial and banking sector, including legal consultations related to financing, loans, bank guarantees, mergers and acquisitions, and compliance with financial regulations.

Labor & Employment

We provide specialized legal services in labor and employment law, including drafting employment contracts and internal company regulations, resolving labor disputes, and legal consultations related to workers' and employers' rights.

Healthcare & Insurance

We specialize in providing legal services to the healthcare and insurance sector, including legal consultations related to medical licenses, health insurance contracts, insurance claims, and medical liability.
